Privacy Policy

We collect the minimum data needed to power quest progress, analytics, and secure wallet experiences.

Effective: January 15, 2025 Scope: QuestLayer web app

1. Data we collect

  • Wallet addresses and connection status.
  • Quest progress, XP, and streak activity.
  • Project configuration details you provide.

2. How we use data

  • Render widgets and track task completion.
  • Provide analytics inside your dashboard.
  • Secure accounts and prevent abuse.

3. Analytics

We use analytics to understand traffic and product usage. This helps improve performance and conversions.

4. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with service providers that help us run QuestLayer, or when required by law.

5. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect data. Wallet login information is handled by wallet providers.

6. Data retention

We retain data only as long as needed to provide services or meet legal requirements.

7. Your choices

You can disconnect wallets, delete projects, or request data export by contacting us.

8. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email privacy@questlayer.app.
