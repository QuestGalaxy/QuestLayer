FAQ

Does the Web3 quest widget support wallet login?

Yes. Wallet login is built in and optional per widget.

Can I restrict quests to NFT holders?

Yes. NFT gating lets you limit quests to specific collections.

How is on-chain activity validated?

QuestLayer can link tasks to wallet actions or off-chain proofs.

Which chains are supported?

Wallet login supports major EVM chains and can expand with AppKit settings.
